Soul Hub

Soul Hub is a Advanced Roblox script for your favourite games! We support many roblox games including Lumber Tycoon 2, Prison Life 2, Broken Bones IV, as well as our Universal Game support along with way more games! Soul Hub initially started off as a Executor and began to grow, sadly we had to discontinue due to lack of funds. Luckily we could revive soul as a script and ensure our users a Safe environment with a guarantee of our script working almost 100% of the time! If you ever come accross a bug please notify our team/devs so we can begin working on a fix to maintain that guarantee, Thank You For Choosing Soul!
